Tecom Group Initial Public Offering
Offering

Enabler of Dubai and the region’s knowledge-based economy will be listing XX% of its shares on the Dubai Financial Markets.

TECOM Group PJSC (“TECOM” or “the Company” or “the Group”), has formally announced its intention to float [20%] of its issued share capital on the Dubai Financial Market (“DFM”) through an Initial Show more Public Offering (“IPO”). TECOM Group is a key contributor to Dubai’s economic diversification and development, attracting leading corporations, entrepreneurs, and specialised talent. Our integrated portfolio of high-quality assets across 10 sector -focused business districts strategically located throughout Dubai cater to 6 vital economic sectors. We create sustainable, vibrant ecosystems for our community members to thrive and collaborate in. Our array of bespoke commercial real estate solutions and value-added services are underpinned by state-of-the-art infrastructure, an enabling regulatory framework, seamless digital portals and innovative working models to meet the needs of our business partners and to future-proof our business in an evolving work environment. TECOM Group ESG strategic initiatives
Our Group-wide ESG strategic initiatives are aligned with the United Nation’s Sustainable Development Goals and 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. They include:
  • Solid ESG framework based on insights from internal and external stakeholders through our 2022 Materiality Assessment
  • Award-winning health, safety and environmental governance model with annual and measurable long-term environmental goals, emissions targets and sustainability initiatives
  • Energy audits and the implementation of a portfolio-wide utility monitoring and analytics platform to monitor energy and water consumption
  • Projects for reducing energy consumption and operational carbon footprint, as well as investments in low-carbon energy sources
  • Group-wide environmental policy and strict operating guidelines to limit the impact on the land and the surrounding areas in which we operate
  • Sector-focused community investment programmes and policies as well as volunteering efforts
  • Incubators and support for start-ups, SMEs and freelancers to nurture talent and innovation in Dubai
  • Experienced Board of Directors and an effective and transparent governance framework in line with international standards and regulatory requirements
  • Additional non-mandatory Risk Committee that oversees ESG matters to ensure TECOM fulfils its responsibilities towards our stakeholders
  • Transparent annual reporting on ESG as per the ESG reporting guidelines of the Securities and Commodities Authority (SCA)

 What is TECOM?
TECOM Group PJSC (“TECOM Group” or “Group”) has been developing strategic, sector-focused business districts across the emirate of Dubai since 1999. As part of Dubai Holding, TECOM Group is committed to enabling Dubai’s economic diversification and development, attracting leading corporations, entrepreneurs, and specialised talent.

 What are the business districts in TECOM’s portfolio
The TECOM Group portfolio consists of ten business districts strategically located across Dubai catering to six vital knowledge-based economic sectors including design, education, manufacturing, media, science, and technology. The Group provides a varied and tailor-made portfolio to its 7,800 customers including offices, co-working spaces, warehouses, as well as land lease. It also provides industry specialised facilities including media production facilities, laboratories, higher education campuses. In total TECOM Group’s land properties cover a total of 163.7 million square feet, consisting of 124.4 million square feet of land leases and 39.3 million square feet of land bank.
